Demo notice. This is a demo application. Data is sourced via olyport.com (which surfaces BLS LAUS county/state labor data and O*NET occupational profiles through MCP servers). Not for production decision-making.
Method note. BLS does not publish unemployment rates broken out by profession at the county level. This dashboard combines what the BLS feed can measure (Lane County + Oregon overall labor stats) with O*NET occupation profiles to flag a watchlist of professions: those tagged Bright Outlook by O*NET (lower attrition risk) versus those not tagged (commonly pressured occupations — automation, e-commerce, industry decline). Treat this as directional, not as a county-level occupational unemployment ranking.
